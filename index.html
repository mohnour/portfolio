Sakakini Palace

Brand Identity — Cairo, Egypt

Sakakini Palace is a masterpiece of architectural eclecticism, yet its intricate details are often lost in complex photographs. The goal was to capture the grand, awe-inspiring feeling of looking up at the central dome — distilling that heavy, historical architecture into a minimal, scalable, modern geometric logo without losing its cultural soul.

Architecture to identity: the Sakakini dome.

El Moez Street

Brand Identity — Islamic Cairo, Egypt

El Moez Street carries immense historical and visual weight. Rather than a generic icon or a literal drawing of a building, the challenge was to design a custom typographic logomark — where the Arabic letters themselves reflect the verticality and structural elegance of the street.

Carried across a full stationery system — business cards, letterhead, envelopes, and notebook — using the mark's own geometry as a repeating pattern.
